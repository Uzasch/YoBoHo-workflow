  ---
  ## Executive Summary

  YoBoHo is a YouTube-first content company managing 10+ active channels with internal and external production. As production scales, the biggest operational challenge is **transparency** - knowing what's happening across the production pipeline and when content is ready for channel managers to upload.

  Currently, handoffs between Writers, Editors, and Channel Managers happen via email. This creates blind spots, delays, and content falling through the cracks. Leadership lacks a unified view of production status across all IPs.

  This internal tool solves that by providing a **visibility engine for content production** - a centralized platform where every team member sees their work, every manager sees production status, and nothing gets lost between handoffs.

  ---

  ## Core Vision

  ### Problem Statement

  YoBoHo's production team lacks transparency across the content pipeline. With 10+ channels actively producing, there's no single source of truth for:
  - What stage each piece of content is in
  - Who is working on what
  - When content is ready for the next stage
  - What's blocked, delayed, or falling through the cracks

  ### Problem Impact

  - **Lost time:** Managers spend hours chasing status updates via email
  - **Missed handoffs:** Content sits waiting because the next person wasn't notified
  - **No clarity:** Monday mornings start with confusion instead of action
  - **Scaling pain:** As channels grow, email-based coordination breaks down further

  ### Why Existing Solutions Fall Short

  Generic project management tools (Asana, Monday, Notion) aren't built for content production pipelines. They lack:
  - **Process-specific workflows** (scripting → editing → publishing)
  - **Smart dependencies** (partial handoffs, parallel work)
  - **Production-focused views** (what's ready to upload, who's behind)
  - **IP-centric organization** (view by channel/show, not just by task)

  ### Proposed Solution

  An internal workflow management platform purpose-built for YoBoHo's content production:
  - **Canvas:** Design visual workflows with drag-drop process stages
  - **Dashboard:** Personal work view + observatory for team awareness
  - **Smart Dependencies:** Linear, partial, and simultaneous handoffs
  - **Notifications:** Email, WhatsApp, in-app alerts when work is ready
  - **AI Assistant:** Answer "what's happening in [IP]?" instantly

  ### Key Differentiators

  1. **Built for content production** - not adapted from generic project tools
  2. **IP-first hierarchy** - organized by channels/shows, not just tasks
  3. **Smart handoffs** - partial completion triggers, parallel workflows
  4. **Visibility engine** - answers "what's happening?" at a glance
  5. **Internal tool** - tailored to YoBoHo's exact processes, no compromises
